## 7. Risks Requiring Founder Attention

### Scope creep

The backlog contains many attractive ideas. None should enter the prototype unless the PRD is formally amended.

### Visual inconsistency

Existing concept images contain multiple Twinko proportions, faces, and styles. A model sheet is required before generating more assets.

### Feature-zoo risk

Chat, Tarot, and Astrology must feel like Twinko’s modes, not three unrelated apps.

### Misleading validation

A polished prototype may look market-ready but does not prove retention, willingness to pay, or health benefit.

### Spiritual-content risk

Tarot and astrology wording can accidentally imply certainty, mind-reading, financial advice, or prediction.

### Emotional-safety risk

Even scripted Chat should avoid implying diagnosis, guaranteed emotional improvement, or exclusive dependency.
