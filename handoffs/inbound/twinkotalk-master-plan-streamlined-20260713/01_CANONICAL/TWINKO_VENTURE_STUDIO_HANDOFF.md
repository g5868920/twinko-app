## Venture Summary

TwinkoTalk is the Venture Studio’s character-first consumer AI companion venture. It centers on Twinko, a glowing yellow star who accompanies users through conversation, tarot reflection, daily astrology, and future wellness / content modes.

The long-term opportunity includes companion subscription, character IP, premium content, cosmetics, creator partnerships, and selected offline wellness-activity referral. None of these commercial options is validated.

## Current Stage

**Prototype definition locked; pre-production.**

The current prototype is Traditional-Chinese-first and includes:

- Welcome.
- Profile Setup: name, birthday, gender.
- Home with five visible modes.
- Active: Chat, Tarot, Astrology.
- Disabled: Meditation, Music.
- Scripted Chat with real Chat History.
- Tarot: single-card and three-card.
- Astrology: overall daily insight, love, career, finance, lucky number, color, sign, item.

No live LLM, authentication, gamification, payments, notifications, or marketplace is included.

## Strategic Role in the Venture Studio

TwinkoTalk serves two roles:

1. A real consumer venture intended to become a commercial product.
2. A future real-world case for the AI-Native PM Workflow after both products are sufficiently mature.

The immediate TwinkoTalk work should not be blocked by PM Workflow dogfooding. Dogfooding resumes after the prototype creates sufficiently structured product, design, content, QA, and status artifacts.

## Current Objective

Prepare and produce a bounded, coherent prototype that proves the intended experience:

- Twinko is memorable.
- Chat feels warm.
- Tarot feels mysterious and ritual-based.
- Astrology provides an understandable daily-return concept.
- All modes belong to one companion world.

## Founder Decisions Already Made

- Name: TwinkoTalk.
- Character: Twinko.
- Traditional Chinese first; English later.
- No account authentication in prototype.
- Active prototype modes: Chat, Tarot, Astrology.
- Meditation and Music visible but inactive.
- No LLM.
- Real Chat History.
- Tarot single and three-card.
- Astrology fields locked.
- Gamification and all other features moved to backlog.
- Latest supplied character image is the visual baseline.

## Top Risks

- Scope creep from the large feature universe.
- Inconsistent Twinko art.
- Three modes feeling like separate apps.
- Overclaiming validation from a prototype.
- Unsafe medical or spiritual claims.
- Future gamification drifting into guilt or dependency.
