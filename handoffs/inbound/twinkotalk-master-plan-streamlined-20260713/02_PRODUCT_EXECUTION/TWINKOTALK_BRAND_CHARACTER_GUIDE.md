# TWINKOTALK_BRAND_CHARACTER_GUIDE

> Version: 0.8 founder-reference draft  
> Status date: 2026-07-13  
> Master visual reference: `08_REFERENCES/assets/twinko_master_visual_reference.png`

## 1. Naming

- App: **TwinkoTalk**.
- Character: **Twinko**.

## 2. Brand Essence

A warm star friend who accompanies the user through conversation, reflection, and small daily rituals.

### Attributes

- Warm.
- Gentle.
- Magical.
- Cosmic.
- Cute.
- Safe.
- Premium.
- Memorable.

### Tensions to balance

- Cute, not childish.
- Mystical, not cheap occult.
- Warm, not sentimental manipulation.
- Premium, not cold.
- Helpful, not clinical.

## 3. Twinko Character Baseline

### Shape

- Five-point star.
- Rounded, soft, full proportions.
- Slightly plush / glowing volume.
- No thin or sharp points.
- No prominent human limbs in the base form.

### Face

- Large black oval eyes.
- Small white highlights.
- Simple upward curved mouth.
- Orange-red round cheeks.
- Friendly neutral expression.

### Material and light

- Golden yellow body.
- Orange warmth near edges / lower body.
- Soft internal glow.
- Gentle bloom, not harsh neon.

### Consistency rules

Do not change between screens:

- Point count.
- Eye shape.
- Mouth style.
- Cheek placement.
- Overall width / height ratio.
- Glow character.

## 4. Character States

Prototype may require:

- Neutral welcome.
- Listening.
- Thinking / loading.
- Happy / encouraging.
- Gentle concern.
- Tarot wizard.
- Astrology guide.

Avoid exaggerated sadness that makes the user feel responsible.

## 5. Home Visual Direction

- Twinko central and largest.
- Pink / lavender / warm orange sky.
- Soft clouds.
- Small gold stars.
- Circular mode icons around Twinko.
- White, rounded typography.
- Chat, Tarot, Astrology active.
- Meditation and Music visually disabled.

## 6. Mode Art Direction

### Chat

- Warm night environment.
- Fireplace, sofa, window, or soft room references.
- Twinko feels physically present.
- Deep navy / warm orange contrast.

### Tarot

- Deep violet / indigo.
- Gold stars and accents.
- Wizard hat / cloak.
- Table and cards.
- Ritual pacing.
- Founder-provided card style should guide the prototype deck.

### Astrology

- Cosmic background.
- Clear information cards.
- Star / constellation detail.
- Avoid generic corporate dashboard feeling.

### Disabled Meditation / Music

- Lower opacity or lock / coming-soon badge.
- Maintain aesthetic coherence.
- Never appear broken.

## 7. Color Direction

Working palette families:

- Twinko Gold.
- Warm Orange.
- Lavender.
- Pink-lavender sky.
- Deep Cosmic Purple.
- Midnight Navy.
- Soft White.

Exact tokens require visual and accessibility testing.

## 8. Typography

Requirements:

- Rounded and friendly.
- Legible in Traditional Chinese and English.
- Avoid excessive outline / glow effects.
- Clear size hierarchy.
- Support long Chinese text without crowding.

Historical Fredoka reference may inform English tone, but final bilingual typography must be tested.

## 9. UI Component Direction

- Rounded cards.
- Clear primary buttons.
- Minimal borders.
- Soft glow used selectively.
- Strong readable contrast.
- Avoid giant text blocks over illustrations.
- Keep touch targets clear.

## 10. Do / Don’t

### Do

- Keep Twinko centered.
- Use mode-specific clothing / environment.
- Preserve face and silhouette.
- Use warmth and negative space.
- Create original art.

### Don’t

- Turn Twinko into a yellow circle.
- Use a different face on every screen.
- Copy protected animation / film styles.
- Overload screens with cards and text.
- Make Tarot copy look like guaranteed prediction.
- Use visual sadness to guilt users.
