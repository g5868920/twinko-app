## Prioritization Criteria for Later

- User need frequency.
- Pain intensity.
- Fit with Twinko relationship.
- Repeat-value potential.
- Safety and privacy risk.
- Content and art cost.
- Operational complexity.
- Revenue potential.
- Evidence quality.
- Ability to test one mechanic at a time.
