## 2. Information-routing rules

- Vision, market, business and product-strategy detail → Product Operating Brief.
- Founder decisions and evidence status → Decision Log.
- Current stage, blockers, execution sequence and change control → Current Status.
- Prototype requirements and acceptance criteria → Prototype PRD.
- Future ideas and gamification concepts → Feature Backlog.
- Character, palette, modes and visual consistency → Brand Character Guide.
- Chat, Tarot, Astrology and UI copy → Content Spec.
- Emotional safety, dependency, spiritual claims, privacy and monetization ethics → Trust/Safety/Legal file.
- Provenance and superseded material → References only.
