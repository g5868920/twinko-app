# TWINKO_CROSS_CHAT_DISCREPANCY_REPORT

> Status date: 2026-07-13  
> Compared sources: original product chat, Market Analysis, Business Model, Gamification / Octalysis, later founder prototype decisions.

## 1. Summary

The core vision was consistent: a character-first companion combining emotional support, Tarot, Astrology, and future wellness content. Most discrepancies came from older long-term ideas being written as immediate MVP requirements, and from hypotheses being written too strongly.

## 3. Important Non-Conflicts

- Character-first product.
- Emotional companionship and reflection.
- Tarot / Astrology as companion modes.
- Cute, warm, cosmic brand.
- Non-medical positioning.
- No guilt or dark patterns.
- Future IP, outfits, and environments.
- Future professional / activity bridge as an option.
